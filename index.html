TOXIC TITLE

Toxic: A Fairy Tale for Grown-Ups is an upcoming Indian period gangster film written by Geetu Mohandas and Yash and directed by Geetu Mohandas.[1] The film features an ensemble cast of Yash, Nayanthara, Kiara Advani, Tara Sutaria, Huma Qureshi, Rukmini Vasanth, Akshay Oberoi and Sudev Nair. It is jointly produced by Venkat K. Narayana and Yash under their KVN Productions and Monster Mind Creations production companies.

TOXIC BIRTHDAY PEEK

The film was officially announced in December 2023 under the tentative title Yash 19, as it is the 19th film of Yash as the leading actor, and the official title was announced a few days later. Principal photography commenced the following August in Bengaluru. The cinematography was handled by Rajeev Ravi. The film was simultaneously shot in Kannada and English and will be released in six languages on 19 March 2026, coinciding with Ugadi.
